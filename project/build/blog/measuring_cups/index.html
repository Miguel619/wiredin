Why I Love Having Lots of Measuring Cups

by Patrick Kennedy

Published on January 8th, 2021

I have a collection of measuring cups and I use them almost every day!

I've found that measuring cups are great for lots of things in the kitchen besides measuring, such as:

  • Use as a small mixing bowl
  • Use for scrambling eggs
  • Use of melting butter in the microwave
  • Use as small bowls when pouring into a stand mixer

I have found three sizes of measuring cups to be ideal:

  • 4 Cups (large)
  • 2 Cups (medium)
  • 1 Cup (small)

The brand of measuring cups that I like is Pyrex. There is a set of 3 Pyrex measuring cups available for purchase, such as from Target: Pyrex Measuring Cup Set 3 piece (non-affiliate link).
